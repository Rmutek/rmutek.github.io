PROJECT

Oy’ster
Oy’ster is a social network for individuals who love, enjoy and are enthusiastic about oysters. Users can post reviews, browse restaurants, find oyster specific specials and receive recommendations. Restaurant roulette is a feature which asks the user for several parameters and then matches them with a restaurant and a specific oyster. As users post reviews they become eligible for exclusive events and special offers. Oy’ster was built using Ruby on Rails and VueJS. Oy’ster was created to share and track oysters enjoyed over a lifetime for those who like to live life on the half shell. Visit Oy’ster at https://shrouded-basin-69875.herokuapp.com/ (Please note that several bugs are still being worked out and I am happy to demo the application locally on my laptop)
